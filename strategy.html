Roadmap to 2030: Empowering 1 Million Change Leaders

Creating Transformational Change through Education, Skills, and Employment.

Our Mission for 2030

Karta Initiative’s Strategy Plan (2024–2030) outlines a bold vision to empower 1 million young people in the Global South through education, skill-building, and employment opportunities.

Key Strategies

  • Equip young people with 21st-century skills such as critical thinking, digital literacy, and problem-solving.
  • Provide scholarships and mentorship to support higher education access for marginalized youth.
  • Facilitate employment opportunities and entrepreneurship development for sustainable careers.
  • Develop youth leaders who will drive social change in their communities.
  • Utilize technology and innovation to deliver scalable education and career solutions.

Long-Term Goals (2024–2030)

  • Train 1 million young people in 21st-century skills.
  • Enable 100,000 youth to access higher education through scholarships and mentorship.
  • Create 100,000 employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.
  • Empower 50,000 young people as social change leaders.
