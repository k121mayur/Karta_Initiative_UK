Karta Initiative’s Strategy Plan (2024–2030)

At Karta Initiative, we believe in creating sustainable, long-term impact for marginalized youth in the Global South. Our Strategy Plan for 2024–2030 outlines a bold vision to empower 1 million young people through innovative, tech-driven, and scalable interventions. This roadmap focuses on bridging critical gaps in education, skill-building, and employment to ensure young people, particularly girls and young women, have the tools to transform their futures.

The strategy emphasizes key priorities:

  1. Building 21st-Century Skills: Equipping youth with digital literacy, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills to prepare them for higher education and careers.
  2. Higher Education Access: Expanding scholarships, mentorship programs, and partnerships with top universities globally to ensure equitable opportunities.
  3. Employment and Entrepreneurship: Facilitating meaningful jobs and supporting youth in launching enterprises that address challenges in their communities.
  4. Youth Leadership: Developing social change leaders who uplift their communities and create a ripple effect of transformation.

Our approach combines technology and partnerships with local and global organizations to deliver impactful programs. By 2030, we aim to achieve ambitious targets, including:

  • Training 1 million youth with essential 21st-century skills.
  • Supporting 100,000 young people in accessing higher education.
  • Creating 100,000 employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.
  • Empowering 50,000 youth as social change leaders.

The Strategy Plan serves as a guide to expand our reach, deepen our impact, and scale our efforts to create opportunities for marginalized communities across India, Nepal, South Africa, and Latin America.

We invite you to join us in this transformative journey, working together to break the intergenerational cycle of marginalization and build a future of equal opportunities for all.
