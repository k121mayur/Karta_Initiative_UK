Our Impact

Transforming lives through education, mentorship, and opportunities.

Making a Difference

Karta Initiative has impacted over 20,000 youth, supporting scholars, providing mentorship, and equipping young people with 21st-century skills.

Karta Catalyst Fund

Supported 260 scholars in accessing higher education at premier universities globally.

Built a mentorship network to support scholars' academic and professional journeys.

Building 21st-Century Skills

Trained more than 10,000 students in critical skills such as digital literacy, communication, and problem-solving.

Prepared marginalized youth for higher education and successful career transitions.

Expanding Partnerships

Collaborated with top global universities including Oxford, Cambridge, UCL, University of Toronto, and more.

Strengthened partnerships in India, Nepal, South Africa, and Latin America.

Impact Stories

Scholars from rural and marginalized communities have overcome barriers, gained quality education, and returned to uplift their communities.

Our programs continue to inspire future generations to break cycles of poverty and marginalization.
