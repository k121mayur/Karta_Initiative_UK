Impact

Karta Catalyst Fund

The Karta Catalyst Fund has supported 260 scholars to date, enabling them to secure admissions to premier universities globally and providing a robust mentorship network. Many of these scholars have successfully graduated and are now poised to drive meaningful change within their communities. Through this initiative, scholars are not only breaking barriers to access higher education but also becoming role models and change leaders in their societies.

Building 21st-Century Skills

Through Karta’s skill-building programs, more than 10,000 students have been trained to date, helping them transition from school to university equipped with 21st-century skills, career guidance, and mentorship. These programs continue to empower youth with essential skills like digital literacy, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork, enabling them to thrive in education and their careers. By equipping marginalized youth with these skills, Karta is preparing the next generation to compete and excel in a rapidly changing global landscape.

Expanding Partnerships

Karta Initiative has built strong collaborations with leading institutions worldwide to provide exceptional opportunities for its scholars.

  • In India, we partner with Flame University, Ashoka University, KREA University, Plaksha University, and Ahmedabad University.
  • In the UK, we work with prestigious institutions such as Oxford University, Cambridge University, University College London (UCL), University of Edinburgh, and University of Bristol.
  • In Canada, our collaborations extend to Queen’s University, McGill University, University of Toronto, and Western University.
