About Us

Bridging Opportunity Gaps for Marginalized Youth

The Karta Initiative is a UK-registered charity (CIO Registered Charity Number: 1173020) committed to addressing significant disparities in access to higher education, 21st-century skills, employment, and entrepreneurship opportunities for young people in the Global South. With a particular focus on girls and young women from marginalized communities, Karta is dedicated to breaking the intergenerational cycle of marginalization and transforming lives through equal opportunities.

Founded on the belief that equitable access to quality higher education, long-term mentorship, and robust social and cultural capital is key to empowering marginalized youth, Karta Initiative envisions a world where educational and workplace equality is achievable for all.

Our Purpose

At its core, The Karta Initiative exists to align potential with opportunity. By focusing on young people from marginalized communities, we aim to create pathways for equitable access to education, skills, and opportunities that empower them to break barriers, achieve their dreams, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Work

We focus on four key priorities to empower youth:

  1. Imparting 21st-century skills to prepare young people for success in education and careers.
  2. Improving access to quality higher education for underserved youth.
  3. Providing long-term mentorship to guide students, build networks, and support career pathways.
  4. Promoting meaningful employment aligned with skills and aspirations.
  5. Supporting entrepreneurship to address challenges faced by marginalized communities.

Our Reach

Since our inception, The Karta Initiative has positively impacted over 20,000 rural students in India, working across multiple states and schools to provide scholarships, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities. With growing partnerships, we are expanding our work to Nepal, South African countries, and Latin America.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Firm belief in turning empathy into action.
  • Equity: Committed to fair access for marginalized youth.
  • Empowerment: Enabling young people to lead and inspire change.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with organizations and communities for lasting impact.
  • Innovation: Leveraging technology to overcome barriers in education and employment.

Our Mission for 2030

To empower 1 million young people in the Global South by providing:

  • 21st-century skills to prepare them for higher education and careers.
  • Access to quality higher education through scholarships, mentorship, and partnerships.
  • Opportunities for meaningful employment and entrepreneurship.
  • Support for developing young leaders who will transform their communities.

Key Strategies to Achieve Our Mission

  1. Higher Education Access
    • Equip young people with 21st-century skills such as critical thinking, digital literacy, and problem-solving to prepare them for education and career success.
  2. Higher Education Access
    • Provide scholarships to deserving students from marginalized communities to study in premier universities
    • Partner with global educational institutions to improve access to quality education.
    • Build mentorship programs to guide students through their academic and career journeys.
  3. Employment and Entrepreneurship
    • Facilitate job opportunities aligned with youth skills and aspirations by collaborating with industries and stakeholders.
    • Support young people in launching their own businesses through financial and resource-based assistance.
  4. Youth Leadership and Community Impact
    • Develop young leaders as social change agents to uplift their communities.
    • Promote youth participation in addressing local and global challenges through innovation and leadership.
  5. Technology and Innovation
    • Leverage technology to deliver scalable and accessible solutions such as digital platforms for skill-building, career resources, and mentorship.
