Bridging Opportunity Gaps for Marginalized Youth

Empowering young people through education, skills, and career opportunities.

Who We Are

The Karta Initiative is a UK-registered charity (CIO Registered Charity Number: 1173020) committed to addressing disparities in access to higher education, skill-building, and employment opportunities for marginalized youth, especially girls and young women.

Our Purpose

At its core, Karta exists to align potential with opportunity. By focusing on young people from marginalized communities, we create pathways for equitable access to education and career opportunities, breaking cycles of intergenerational disadvantage.

Who We Serve

Our target group includes young people aged 14 to 25 from marginalized communities in the Global South, particularly girls and young women facing systemic barriers in education and employment.

Our Work

  • Providing 21st-century skills to young people to prepare them for success.
  • Enhancing access to quality higher education through scholarships and mentorship.
  • Promoting meaningful employment aligned with skills and aspirations.
  • Supporting entrepreneurship to address challenges in marginalized communities.

Our Reach

Since our inception, we have impacted over 20,000 rural students in India, expanded our efforts into Nepal, South African countries, and Latin America, and continue to grow our reach globally.
