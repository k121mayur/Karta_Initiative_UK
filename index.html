Karta Initiative

Bridging gaps in education, skills, and opportunities for young people.

Empowering Marginalized Youth in the Global South

At the Karta Initiative, we bridge gaps in education, skills, and opportunities for young people from

underserved communities, transforming lives and building brighter futures.

Transforming Marginalized Communities Through Education and Skills

The Karta Initiative is a UK-registered charity (CIO Registered Charity Number: 1173020) dedicated to marginalized youth, particularly girls and young women, with access to quality higher education, 21st-century skills, global mentorship, career readiness and meaningful career opportunities.
